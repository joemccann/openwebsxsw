Hello from The Open Web SXSW Team!


So you've registered, but you know little about the party, right? What gives?!
 
Just like last year, we're doing our best to keep this event unique and new so keeping you uninformed is part of that process!
 
But don't fret, we'll give you a little bit of info now.
  The party begins at 3pm on Sunday, March 10th. Because the weather is playing a game of chicken with us, we decided to move it inside, away from our actual pool, but to a very "beachy" environment.
 
 
 

What's Next?


Due to an absolutely staggering amount of demand, we have a 2-part system for entry. First, there will be a list of people whom have been selected from our lottery system. If you were lucky enough to end up in the lottery, you'll receive an email notifying you of your luck! If not, you will receive an email notifying you what to do so you still have a shot of getting into the party. Our venue is large, but not big enough for 37,532 people!
